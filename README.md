# Bastion

> *You are the scavenger. The one who goes out into the dangerous world and brings things back. The community depends on you. You depend on the community.*

---

Build 42 added a lot of grind. Picking thread from rags. Boiling bandages. Checking trap lines. Watering every crop plot by hand. The Indie Stone said as much themselves — that work was designed to be handed off to NPCs. Bastion gives you those NPCs now.

Your settlers have names. They have backstories. They have a reason to be in your building and not somewhere else. When things go wrong — when the food runs short or someone doesn't make it back — you feel it, because you know who it happened to.

They take over the repetitive work so you can focus on the part that's actually interesting: going out, finding things, coming back alive.

---

## What it does

A settlement runs in the background while you play. Settlers consume real food and water from your storage. They do real work — producing items, maintaining equipment, keeping things running. A prioritized work queue figures out what matters most each day and gets it done first. If water is critically low, that jumps to the top. If thread is already stocked, it waits.

The settlement grows. More settlers means a larger radius, more capability, more things that can go wrong in interesting ways.

---

## Early development

Bastion is actively being designed and built. The simulation loop, the item economy, and the settler system are in progress. Walking NPCs are on the roadmap — the Bandits mod proved it's possible, and we're getting there.
